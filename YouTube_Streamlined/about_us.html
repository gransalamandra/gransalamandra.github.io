About us!

History

Made in 2005, Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim create YouTube as a video sharing platform. As a first of its kind it aimed to provide an easy to use and upload site for the general public.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to give everyone a voice and show them the world.

Company Overview/Description

A video sharing platform available to the entire world. YouTube allows communities to connect and for the easy upload of videos.
