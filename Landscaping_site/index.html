Our History

JM Landscaping, Inc. is a full-service landscape firm (2022)serving the Houston area, From the maintenance and enhancement of your existing property, to the design and installation of the outdoor areas for your new home, JM Landscaping is here to serve your landscape needs.

Services

We offer a wide and comprehensive range of premium landscaping services, just see for yourself.

Custom Landscape Design

Our Custom Landscape Design service is a personalized journey to bring your outdoor vision to life. We collaborate closely with you to understand your preferences, lifestyle, and the unique features of your property. Our expert designers integrate artistic flair with practicality, ensuring that every element complements your taste and enhances the overall aesthetic of your space. Whether you envision a tranquil retreat, a vibrant floral paradise, or a modern oasis, our Custom Landscape Design service tailors the outdoor experience to reflect your individual style.

Lawn Care and Maintenance

Immerse yourself in the luxury of a lush, green lawn all year round with our comprehensive Lawn Care and Maintenance service. Our skilled team takes a proactive approach to keep your grass healthy and vibrant. From regular mowing and fertilization to weed control and aeration, we provide the essential care your lawn needs. Our services extend beyond mere maintenance; we aim to create a thriving ecosystem in your outdoor space, ensuring your lawn becomes a focal point of beauty and comfort.

Hardscaping

Elevate the functionality and aesthetics of your outdoor space with our Hardscaping service. Our skilled craftsmen design and install durable and visually stunning hardscape features, including paths, patios, retaining walls, and more. We blend form and function seamlessly, ensuring that each element not only enhances the overall design but also serves a practical purpose. Whether you desire a charming stone pathway, an inviting patio for entertaining, or a structurally sound retaining wall, our Hardscaping service transforms your outdoor environment into a functional and captivating retreat.

Irrigation Systems

Experience smart and efficient water management with our Irrigation Systems service. We design and install state-of-the-art irrigation systems tailored to the specific needs of your landscape. Our systems use advanced technology to optimize water distribution, ensuring that every part of your garden receives the right amount of moisture. From smart controllers that adjust based on weather conditions to precision-engineered drip systems, our Irrigation Systems service not only conserves water but also promotes the health and longevity of your plants.

Seasonal Planting

Embrace the ever-changing beauty of your garden with our Seasonal Planting service. Our horticulturists carefully select and strategically plant seasonal flowers, shrubs, and trees to ensure a constantly evolving and vibrant landscape. Whether it's the burst of spring blooms, the lushness of summer greenery, the warm tones of autumn foliage, or the hardiness of winter flora, our Seasonal Planting service ensures your garden is in bloom year-round. We curate a palette of plants that thrive in each season, creating a dynamic and visually appealing outdoor space.
